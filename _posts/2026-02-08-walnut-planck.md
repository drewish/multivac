I wanted to show off the wooden keyboard case case I made.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/00-final.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

I got a nice piece walnut from another member of the [maker space](https://www.ssdmakerspace.org).
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/01-stock.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="" >

For the first operations I used the 1/2" bit to remove most of the material and then faced the top and sides. With the [painters tape and super glue](/2026/02/01/cnc-work-holding/) holding it down, I'm able to cut all the way to the spoil board.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/02-back-1o2.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

Drilled out the screw holes.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/03-back-drill.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

Put a nice 45Â° chamfer on all the edges.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/04-back-chamfer.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

For the USB cutout on the back, I put it in a vice.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/05-back-vice.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

I forgot to get a picture after that finished so here's one from later in the process that shows how nicely the wood held the detail.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/11-usb-cutout-detail.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

Now we flip it over and hog out the inside. I felt bad turning such nice wood into chips.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/06-top-1o2-action.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

I used a 1/8" bit to hollow out the depression for the USB port. It was interesting to see the difference in the finish between what that and the 1/2" bit left.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/07-top-usb-detail.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

Put a chamfer on the top edges and we're done.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/09-top-chamfer.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

Now I'm ready to start sanding.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/10-back-unfinished.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

Used some [Rowdy Rooster hard wax oil](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DS1BVWGC) to finish it.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/12-top-finished.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

I threw some rubber bumpers on the corners and mounted the PCB.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/16-bottom-finished.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

The clearance on the USB isn't quite right. I'll adjust that if I do another version.
<img src="{{ 'assets/images/blog/walnut-planck/17-usb-cutout-alignment.jpeg' | resize: '640x640>' }}" alt="">

Other than that I'm very happy with how it turned out.
